Our Medical Credentialing Process

A Comprehensive Overview of Our Specialist-Vetted Provider Credentialing Process

At Profound Medical Billing Inc., our provider credentialing process goes beyond routine procedures. It's a meticulously validated and certified approach that ensures compliance with the highest standards. We boast a success rate of up to 98% in securing providers' approval in premium payer networks with maximum privileges in their specialties. Here’s how we do it:

Provider Survey:

Our process begins with a thorough survey conducted by our credentialing specialists. We conduct in-depth interviews with providers to gather crucial data such as license numbers, educational history, demographics, and professional background.

01

Insurance Partner Selection:

We guide providers in strategically selecting insurance partners aligned with their practice goals and license type. We finalize insurance panels based on the provider's practice location to optimize network participation.

02

CAQH Enrollment and Management:

We handle CAQH application submissions, manage ProView accounts, and ensure credentials are up-to-date for seamless primary source verification. Our team also facilitates payer credentialing submissions to integrate quickly into insurance panels, enhancing network engagement and patient care quality.

03

Accelerated Credentialing Approval:

While typical timelines range from 60-120 days, our credentialing experts proactively expedite the process. We maintain regular communication with payers, advocating for swift processing and approval of credentialing applications.

04

In-Network Enrollment:

Upon credentialing completion, we navigate the contract phase where in-network contracts may be offered, detailing fee schedules and eligible CPT billing codes. We manage challenges related to closed panels, pursuing appeals to secure inclusion. Successful credentialing facilitates direct billing, often with preferred reimbursement rates for in-network providers.

05

Hospital Privileges Application:

We continue to support providers post-credentialing by assisting in securing necessary hospital privileges, whether for admitting, courtesy, or surgical rights.

06

Ongoing Monitoring and Updates:

Our commitment doesn't end with credentialing. We provide continuous oversight to ensure your credentials remain current without interruptions. We monitor credentialing expirations diligently, conducting comprehensive reviews to maintain active status seamlessly.
