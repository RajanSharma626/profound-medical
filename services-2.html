How Does Profound Medical Billing Inc. Help?

Clinical Statement Analysis

Our coders meticulously analyze medical statements and documentation provided by healthcare providers, classifying this information using standardized classifications to ensure accuracy and compliance.

Medical Notes Coding

Expert physician coders translate diagnosis and procedures into easily readable codes for insurance companies, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free process for medical providers.

Super-Bill Submission

Collaborating with the billing team, our coders generate a super bill that outlines the charges the payer is responsible for, including patient insurance coverage and any co-payments.

Claim Approval

Our coders act as advocates for healthcare providers, ensuring claim approval. They focus on recovering aged receivables and working to get denied claims paid, enhancing overall revenue cycle management.
